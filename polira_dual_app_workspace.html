What are the insulation requirements for an exterior wall in Climate Zone 5?
9:42 AM
AI
Under BC Building Code § 9.36.2.4, exterior walls in Climate Zone 5 must meet a minimum effective thermal resistance of RSI 3.85 (R-21.9).

For wood-frame construction this is typically achieved with: R-22 batt in the cavity plus continuous exterior insulation, OR an R-28 advanced framing assembly. Vapour barrier requirements under § 9.25.3 also apply.
